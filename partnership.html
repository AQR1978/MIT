The Partnership

A transformational collaboration bringing world-class nursing education to the region

What This Partnership Means for You

HCT is partnering with the University of Barcelona, one of Europe's most respected institutions, to deliver a globally-recognized Master of Science in Nursing (MSN) program. This is more than an online degree—it's a pathway to advanced healthcare leadership.

  • High-caliber academic partnership with a globally-respected institution
  • Strong local delivery model designed for working professionals
  • Meaningful career positioning for advanced healthcare leadership
  • CHED-aligned framework with clear professional development focus
  • Access to faculty expertise and research opportunities
